🏫 About Us

Redefining What a Preschool Can Be

AFK Preschool is Lucknow's first institution to integrate pediatric therapist expertise into daily classroom life. We believe a child's earliest years are their most formative — and they deserve more than just academic preparation.

Every activity, every routine, every interaction at AFK is designed to support physical, cognitive, and emotional growth through play-based learning guided by professionals.
